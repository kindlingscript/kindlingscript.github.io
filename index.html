Ever since I was a kid, I wanted to be an author. At the age of nine, I started writing Bunnicula fanfiction before I even knew what “fanfiction” was. Later I’d move on to writing original ficton and poetry, of which you can find some examples here. Though I’ve still got a poetry and fiction writing bug inside me (& always will), nowadays, my muse mainly works through writing code and designing for the responsive web.

Drawing upon a degree in psychology, the user experience of website or application is what I work on prioritizing first. The web’s for people - technology is for people - and so they come first.

I’m constantly learning and working on improving my work and myself. Oh, and I enjoy things like comics / graphic novels, video games, synthwave music, and crisp, cool weather. Autumn's my favorite season.
