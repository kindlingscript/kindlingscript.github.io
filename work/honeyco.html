My client wanted a landing page redesign for their startup website, as they felt their current one at the time didn't convey what they wanted.

Using the Sketch app, I used their current logo, hero image, a screenshot of their animated video, and the knowledge of what they do, turning it into this new landing page detailing their mission and what they offer. Content writing in the design is my own (which is why there is a bit of Lorem ipsum for gaps in knowledge). Though this design was purchased, it does not reflect what is currently on their website.
