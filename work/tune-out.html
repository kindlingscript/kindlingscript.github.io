tune out.

Need to relax, kick back and listen to some ambient music while watching a slideshow of natural scenery you pick out? That's the purpose of this application.

Put in a type of scenery you enjoy (such as "mountainside" or "beach"), pick out a genre of music you like from the dropdown menu, and get some results from the Flickr and SoundCloud APIs.
